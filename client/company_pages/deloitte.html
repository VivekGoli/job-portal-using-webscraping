Deloitte

Deloitte Touche Tohmatsu Limited (DTTL), commonly referred to as Deloitte, is a multinational professional services network headquartered in London, United Kingdom. Deloitte is one of the Big Four accounting firms, alongside KPMG, Ernst & Young, and PricewaterhouseCoopers. The company provides audit, tax, consulting, financial advisory, risk advisory, and related services to individuals, businesses, and governments.

Deloitte has been operating in India for over 75 years. The company has a strong presence in India with over 50,000 employees across 20 offices in major cities. Deloitte India is one of the largest professional services firms in India and serves a wide range of clients, including multinational corporations, Indian companies, and government entities.

Deloitte India is committed to corporate social responsibility (CSR) and has a number of CSR initiatives in place. The company's CSR initiatives focus on education, healthcare, and environmental sustainability.

Deloitte India offers a variety of career opportunities for people with a variety of skills and experience. The company is committed to developing its employees and offers a number of training and development programs.
