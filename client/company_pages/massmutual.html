Mass Mutual

The Massachusetts Mutual Life Insurance Company, also known as MassMutual, is a Springfield, Massachusetts-based life insurance company. Founded in 1851, MassMutual provides financial products such as life insurance, disability income insurance, long-term care insurance, retirement/401(k) plan services, and annuities.MassMutual ranks at 100 in the 2022 Fortune 500 list of the largest United States corporations by total revenue. The company has revenues of $10.7 billion and assets under management of $312 billion (as of 2022).
